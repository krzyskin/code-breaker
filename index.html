CODE-BREAKER

The goal of the game is to guess the password. You can choose between playing alone guessing randomized word or try to guess the password entered by the other player. Second player receives information whether the word entered matches the password. Information are displayed in the way shown below. Randomized words contain 5 letters.

one letter appears in the password and is in the right place

one letter appears in the password but is in the wrong place

------------ none of the letters appears in the password to guess
